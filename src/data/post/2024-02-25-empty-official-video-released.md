## North Sea Echoes "Empty" (Official Video)

The third single off the album, **"Really Good Terrible Things"**, titled **_“Empty”_** receives an official video featuring both **Ray Alder** and **Jim Matheos**. When asked what fans can expect from the new track, **Alder** keeps it short and not-so-sweet, _“I think this one is pretty dark.”_

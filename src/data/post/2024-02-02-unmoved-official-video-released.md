## North Sea Echoes "Unmoved" (Official Video)

The second single off the album, **"Really Good Terrible Things"**, titled **_“Unmoved”_** receives an official video featuring both **Ray Alder** and **Jim Matheos**. Speaking on the lyrical concepts behind **_"Unmoved"_**, **Ray Alder** shares: _"Some people dealing with depression often exhibit a remarkable ability to conceal their inner struggles by presenting a façade of happiness to the world."_

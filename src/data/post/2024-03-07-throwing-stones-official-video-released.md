## North Sea Echoes "Throwing Stones" (Official Video)

The fourth single off the album, **"Really Good Terrible Things"**, titled ***“Throwing Stones”*** receives an animated official video created by [Patrick Atkins](https://www.patrickatkins.co.uk/). Visit [this link to his website](https://www.patrickatkins.co.uk/#/throwingstones/) to view storyboards from the video.

Patrick also created the artwork for [Fates Warning](https://fateswarning.com/)'s album "Long Day Good Night" and an animated video for the song "Begin Again"

On **"Throwing Stones"**, **Ray Alder** shares, *"This is a strange one. Some people have a fear of happiness. They feel that something painful always follows pleasure. So they're sort of locked into this world where they try to feel nothing. It's called Cherophobia."*

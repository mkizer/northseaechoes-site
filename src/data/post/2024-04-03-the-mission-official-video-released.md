## North Sea Echoes "The Mission" (Official Video)

The fifth single off the album, **"Really Good Terrible Things"**, titled ***“The Mission”*** receives an animated official video.

Animation by Nadeera Abeywansha

On the lyrical narrative for ***“The Mission”***, **Ray Alder** shares, *“A man experiences a psychotic episode in which he hears voices. I think this is pretty obvious. This was the last song written for the album. It almost didn’t make it. Jim thought the album was a bit too short so we added it last minute. I’m glad we did. It’s one of my favorites.”*
